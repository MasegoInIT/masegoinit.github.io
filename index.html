Luminators Online Quiz

Welcome to the Ultimate Shoprite Trivia Challenge!

Get ready to embark on an exhilarating journey of knowledge and fun as we test your wit and wisdom about all things Shoprite! Whether you're a seasoned shopper or just love a good challenge, this is your chance to showcase your expertise and compete against friends and colleagues in the ultimate battle of brains.



General Rules:
Team Formation: While every player participates individually, you may designate someone to assist you, but you cannot change your nominee once selected. Choose your nominee wisely.
Scoring System: Each correct answer earns 2 points. If your answer is incorrect but close enough, you will receive half a point.
Answer Submission: Please take note of your answers and the corresponding question numbers.
Limits: Participants have less than 10 seconds to provide an answer. If the time elapses, the question moves to the next player in line.
Fair Play: Cheating is strictly prohibited.
